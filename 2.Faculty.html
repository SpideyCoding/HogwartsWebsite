Cuthbert Binns History of Magic The History of Magic teacher, Cuthbert Binns has the distinction of being the only teacher at Hogwarts who is a ghost. It is said that one day he took a nap in a chair in the staff room and died in his sleep; later, he simply got up to go to teach his next class and "left his body behind". His lessons are notoriously boring and it is mentioned that the most exciting thing that ever happens in his class is his "entering through the blackboard". As a ghost, he seems unaware of his change from living to dead.
Charity Burbage Muggle Studies The Muggle Studies teacher.
Argus Filch Caretaker The Squib caretaker of Hogwarts. He owns a cat named Mrs Norris, who seems to possess certain magical abilities. He hates all students and he is bitter about not possessing magical abilities.
Rubeus Hagrid Care of Magical Creatures The Half-giant gamekeeper, Keeper of Keys and Grounds and the Care of Magical Creatures professor. He has a habit of introducing dangerous creatures, which he considers harmless, to the students.
Rolanda Hooch Flying The Quidditch referee, and flying instructor for first-year students.
Irma Pince Librarian The librarian of Hogwarts. She is very possessive and protective with the library books.
Poppy Pomfrey Matron The Matron and nurse in charge of the hospital wing.
Aurora Sinistra Astronomy The Astronomy teacher.
Septima Vector Arithmancy The Arithmancy teacher, known to give her students large amounts of homework.
